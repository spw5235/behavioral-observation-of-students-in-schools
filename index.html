BOSS Application

The Behavioral Observation System (BOSS) is an observation system designed to objectively and quantifiably record student engagement, and multiple off-task behaviors within a school setting. The BOSS allows for the observer to collect information on the target child as well as information on a peer for comparison and also teacher directed instruction. Behaviors are observed during a total of 60, 15-second intervals.
